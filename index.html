How it works

Monitor

Algorithms will be doing the work for you, automatically reviewing your child’s searches, images, and messages for concerning behavior.

Prompt

If your child crosses a boundary or is exposed to inappropriate content, he/she will be provided the opportunity to change course.

Alert

If the child makes the decision to continue access to the inappropriate content, the parent will be alerted and encouraging a conversation.

Start the conversation

The parent will be prompted with ways to begin the conversation with his/her child and continue to support autonomy and safety.
